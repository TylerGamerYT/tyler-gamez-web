About Tyler's Gamez

Our Mission: We are an indie development team dedicated to creating fun, highly experimental, and endlessly playable experiences. From minimalist clickers to mysterious worlds, we turn simple concepts into unforgettable games that bring the community together.

Our Story

August 24, 2020
Tyler Studios & Early Days
Tyler Studios originally started it all. Tyler paired up with a developer named Icey to build Fighting Kit Royale back in the day. After working together closely, Icey eventually departed the team for unknown reasons, which led Tyler to step back from coding and creating games entirely for a long 2 to 3 year hiatus.
Mid 2025
Meeting Chloe
After taking a break, Tyler crossed paths with Chloe another young coder his age who shared the exact same passion for making unique projects. They instantly became development partners, best friends, and a duo of silly teenagers ready to code something awesome together.
October 11, 2025
Tyler's Gamez Roblox Group Founded
Tyler officially went ahead and registered the group Tyler's Gamez on Roblox. They started out using a silly first concept version for the community logo.
October 20, 2025
The "Chair." Phenomenon
Just nine days later, Tyler and Chloe created a silly, experimental project titled Chair. meant to release as a small project for Halloween. To their absolute surprise, the game went viral overnight clearing over 1,000 visits in roughly a single week, scaling straight up to 3,000, and rapidly rocketing past 10,000+ total plays!
Jan - Feb 2026
"Tree", "Tyler's Games", & "A Button"
Carrying the huge momentum from Chair, Tyler began creating a new game named Tree in January 2026 while managing active side updates. Moving forward, he created the group brand "Tyler's Games" on February 17, 2026, and rolled out an addictive simulator game named A Button on February 26, 2026, focusing on pure, satisfying clicker mechanics!
February 13, 2026
The Launch of bot1hello
Tyler built and launched the bot1hello account on X (formerly Twitter), which ran continually until February 23. Fun Fact: Bot1Hello stands out as one of our longest-running and most enduring active projects today!
April 8, 2026
Expanding Horizons: Bluesky & Mastodon
The bot branched out into the broader network ecosystem, establishing a dedicated presence across alternative social platforms Bluesky and Mastodon.
April 23, 2026
The Return of bot1hello
Following its temporary break, the bot officially came back online and resumed operations, firing back up to share its simple, signature greetings with the digital world once more.
May 1, 2026
The Food Expansion: Foodz Games
Tyler launched a brand new group branch named Foodz Games to completely manage his culinary-themed experiences. This dedicated studio brand hosts hits like Food Clicker 1 and Food Clicker 2. Around this time, he also created a separate side project outside the core Tyler's Gamez lineup called The Food Obby.
May 2, 2026
Bot Localization Updates
Tyler began building out multilingual updates for the bot to adapt it into completely different languages. (Note: These localized expansions remain in development and are not out yet!)
May 20, 2026
Telegram Integration Launch
Expanding its network reach even further, the automated project officially expanded its ecosystem by launching its specialized bot operations on Telegram.
May 27, 2026
Secret Operations: Tyler's Gamez Orange
Looking to venture into brand new game styles, Tyler created an experimental side division group named Tyler's Gamez Orange. This brand is currently keeping some secret, highly anticipated games under wraps that are not publicly released yet!
