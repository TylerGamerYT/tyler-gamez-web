Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Tyler's Gamez

Short version: We don't collect personal data, we don't have accounts, and we don't sell anything about you. This site is just here to showcase our Roblox games.

01 — Overview

Who We Are

Tyler's Gamez is an independent game development project focused on creating games on the Roblox platform. This website (tylergamez.vercel.app) serves as our public homepage and games directory.

We are not a registered company or legal entity Tyler's Gamez is a personal/hobbyist project.

02 - Data Collection

What We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information through this website. Specifically:

  • There are no user accounts or sign-ups
  • We do not run contact forms that store submissions
  • We do not use tracking pixels or ad networks
  • We do not collect your name, email, IP address, or any other identifying information
03 - Third-Party Services

External Platforms

This site is hosted on Vercel, which may collect basic server-side logs (such as IP addresses and request data) as part of normal web hosting operations. This is handled by Vercel under their own privacy policy, which you can review at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Our games are hosted on Roblox. When you play our games on Roblox, Roblox's own privacy policy and terms of service apply. We do not receive personal data from Roblox about players.

This site links to social media platforms including YouTube, Instagram, X (Twitter), and Facebook. Clicking those links takes you to third-party sites governed by their own privacy policies.

04 - Cookies

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not set any cookies of its own. Vercel's hosting infrastructure may set technical cookies necessary for delivering the site. We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or tracking purposes.

05 - Children

Young Visitors

Tyler's Gamez games on Roblox are intended to be enjoyed by players of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal data through this website, there is no special risk to younger visitors browsing here. For data handling within Roblox games themselves, please refer to Roblox's privacy policy.

06 - Changes

Updates to This Policy

If we ever change how this site works in a way that affects privacy (for example, adding a contact form or analytics), we'll update this page with a new date at the top. We recommend checking back if you have concerns.

07 - Contact

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or anything related to Tyler's Gamez, you can reach out via our social media channels linked in the footer of the homepage.
